The Koenigsegg Regera is a high-performance hybrid hypercar developed by Koenigsegg. Introduced in 2015, the Regera combines a 5.0-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine with three electric motors, producing a combined output of around 1,500 horsepower. Unlike conventional supercars, it uses Koenigsegg Direct Drive (KDD) technology instead of a traditional multi-speed gearbox, providing smooth and extremely rapid acceleration. The Regera can accelerate from 0 to 100 km/h in about 2.8 seconds and reach a top speed of over 400 km/h, making it one of the fastest and most technologically advanced production cars in the world. Its luxurious interior, innovative engineering, and limited production make the Regera a highly exclusive and sought-after hypercar.
